Delete My Account

Last updated: July 14, 2026

EN FR

You can request deletion of your Haneul (하늘) account and all associated data at any time, either directly from the app or by contacting us by email.

01Delete your account from the app

  1. Open Haneul and go to the Profile tab
  2. Tap the Settings icon (gear)
  3. Scroll down to the Danger Zone section
  4. Tap Delete my account and confirm

02Can't access the app anymore?

If you're unable to log in to the app, you can request account deletion by emailing haneul.app.contact@gmail.com from the email address associated with your account. We'll process your request within 30 days.

03Data deleted

Permanently deleted

Your email, password, library (series, films, statuses, ratings, personal notes), profile picture, statistics, follow relationships (followers/following), and any community profile content.

Not retained

No personal data is retained after deletion, except where legally required (for example, an ongoing dispute). Data stored locally on your device is removed immediately when the app is uninstalled.

04Processing time

Deletion from within the app is immediate. Requests sent by email are processed within 30 days maximum, in accordance with GDPR.
